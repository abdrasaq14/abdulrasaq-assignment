Tribute to Sir Ahmadu Bello

Sir Ahmadu Ibrahim Bello is one of the most outstanding Nigerian fathers of the nation. He engaged in political activities for over 30 years and was the premier in the Northern Nigeria region for around two decades. The legend and Abubakar Tafawa Balewa played significant roles in the fight for Nigeria's independence.
